About us

Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering

The Department of Civil Engineering at Karpagam Academy of Higher Education in Coimbatore has established a comprehensive Consultancy and Testing wing since January 2018. Their team of experts provides consultancy in structural design, building construction, planning, and documentation. Moreover, five staff members have received certification as registered engineers in various categories, such as Structural Design work, Developer, Geo Technical Engineering, Registered Engineer, and Construction Engineer, from the local planning authority in Coimbatore. They are actively involved in geotechnical investigations, material and water sample testing, and have contributed to Smart City projects in Coimbatore, Tiruppur, Nilgiris, and the Karur Government Medical College. The department is also authorized to issue Third Party Inspection Certificates for town panchayat road works in Sulur, Vellalore, Kinathukadavu, and Madhukarai. Their consultancy and testing services extend to private construction projects, including multistory buildings, commercial complexes, and industrial sheds, making them a valuable resource for both public and private sector projects in the region.
Academic Programs

The Department of Civil Engineering offers a range of academic programs, including a Bachelor of Engineering in Civil Engineering, a Master of Engineering in Structural Engineering, and a Ph.D. program in Civil Engineering. These programs provide students with a comprehensive education.

Specializations

The Department of Civil Engineering specializes in a wide array of areas within the field, including Structural Engineering Geotechnical Engineering, Environmental Engineering,Construction Management and Water Resources Engineering

Laboratory Facilities

The Department of Civil Engineering is equipped with state-of-the-art laboratories, including Geotechnical Engineering Laboratory, Structural Lab, Construction Lab, Survey Lab

Man Power

A team of dedicated faculty members with both industry and academic experience guides students in their educational journey, providing valuable insights and expertise.
