About us

Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering

The Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering offers Bachelor of Engineering in Electrical and Electronics Engineering and Master of Engineering in Power Systems Engineering Research programmes: Ph.D. in Electrical and Electronics Engineering. The specializations of the department are Energy Auditing and survey management, Power Systems, High Voltage Engineering, Power Electronics and Drives, Control and Instrumentation, Embedded System Technology, Electric Drives and Embedded Control. The Department has well-equipped Machines, Power Analyzer, Power Electronics and Power System Laboratories and a team of dedicated faculty members who have experience in the Industry as well as Academic.

Our Services

Powering Progress with Expert Electrical Services. Your Trusted Partner for a Brighter Tomorrow.

01
Electrical Distribution System
An Electrical Distribution System is vital for efficient power transmission in buildings.It comprises transformers, switchgear, and panels, guaranteeing safe and reliable electricity distribution, a core aspect of our electrical consultancy services.
02
PF Improvement Study
Our Power Factor Improvement (PF) Study is a crucial service aimed at enhancing energy efficiency. We analyze and optimize power factor correction solutions, resulting in reduced energy costs and improved system performance.
03
PQ Study / Harmonics
Our Power Quality (PQ) Study and Harmonics Analysis service is dedicated to maintaining the quality and reliability of your electrical systems. Through meticulous analysis and corrective measures, we enhance power quality, reducing issues caused by harmonics.
04
Capacitor performance
Capacitor performance is a vital aspect of electrical systems. Capacitors store and release electrical energy, enhancing power factor and efficiency. Regular evaluation and maintenance of capacitor performance are essential for ensuring a reliable and cost-effective electrical infrastructure.
05
Transformer optimization
Transformer optimization is key to efficient power distribution. We specialize in analyzing and enhancing transformer performance, resulting in reduced energy loss and improved system reliability. Our expertise ensures your electrical infrastructure operates at its best.
06
Cable sizing & loss reduction
Proper cable sizing is essential for minimizing energy loss and ensuring electrical safety. Our service includes meticulous cable sizing to reduce power losses and enhance system efficiency. This is crucial for cost-effective and reliable electrical installations.
07
Motor loading survey
Our Motor Loading Survey is a comprehensive analysis of motor performance. We assess motor efficiency, identify issues, and recommend improvements. This service ensures optimal functionality, reduces energy consumption, and enhances the longevity of your motors.
08
Lighting system
Effective lighting systems are vital for safety and productivity. We design and install energy-efficient lighting solutions, optimizing illumination while minimizing power consumption. Our expertise ensures well-lit and sustainable spaces.
09
Electrical Safety
Electrical safety is paramount. We provide rigorous safety assessments, training, and solutions to prevent electrical hazards. Trust us to keep your electrical systems secure and compliant, safeguarding both people and property.
